About Us

We are a team of students interested in robotics.
With our joint capabilities we benefit from each other, much like the way different foodstuffs in a bento box add up to create something great.

Our current robot is Zyklop, an all-rounded robot that got us into robotics and has been testing ground for many features.
Our next-gen robot is the 'Bento Box', a small 6-wheeled contraption that can map out dangerous areas and search for humans.
